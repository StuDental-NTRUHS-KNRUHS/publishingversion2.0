Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 2024

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, university details, college information, and year of study
  • Payment Information: Transaction reference numbers, payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Academic Information: Course selections, subject preferences, and academic progress
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and website usage patterns
  • Communication Data: Messages, queries, and feedback sent through our contact forms or email

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Course Delivery: Providing access to educational content, live classes, and study materials
  • Communication: Sending course updates, announcements, and responding to queries
  • Payment Processing: Verifying payments and managing enrollment status
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our educational offerings
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and protecting our legal interests
  • Marketing: Sending promotional materials about new courses (with opt-out options)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • We DO NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties
  • Service Providers: Sharing with trusted partners who assist in course delivery (video platforms, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: Disclosing information when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: Information may be transferred in case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: Sharing with your explicit consent for specific purposes

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard security measures and protocols
  • Data Backup: Regular backups with secure storage and recovery procedures

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your personal information

6. Cookies and Tracking

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality and course access
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website usage and improve user experience
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Cookie Control: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings

7. Data Retention

  • Course Duration: Personal information retained for the duration of your enrollment
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained longer to comply with legal obligations
  • Inactive Accounts: Data from inactive accounts may be deleted after 3 years
  • Payment Records: Financial information retained as required by law

8. Third-Party Services

  • Payment Processors: UPI and banking services for secure payment processing
  • Video Platforms: Third-party services for live class delivery
  • Analytics: Website analytics tools to improve user experience
  • Communication: Email and messaging services for course communications

9. International Data Transfers

  • Data Location: Your data is primarily stored and processed in India
  • Cross-Border Transfers: Limited transfers only to countries with adequate protection
  • Safeguards: Appropriate safeguards in place for any international transfers

10. Children's Privacy

  • Age Requirement: Our services are intended for users 18 years and older
  • Parental Consent: Users under 18 must have parental consent
  • Data Protection: Enhanced protection measures for users under 18

11. Updates to Privacy Policy

  • Notification: Users will be notified of significant changes via email
  • Effective Date: Changes become effective 30 days after notification
  • Continued Use: Continued use of services constitutes acceptance of updated policy

12. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests, please contact us:

  • Email: studental.queries@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Privacy Policy Inquiry"
  • Response Time: We will respond within 48 hours

13. Governing Law

  • Jurisdiction: This privacy policy is governed by Indian law
  • Compliance: We comply with applicable Indian data protection regulations
  • Dispute Resolution: Any disputes will be resolved through Indian courts
